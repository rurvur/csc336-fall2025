What the heck's a Metroidvania?


A Metroidvania is a genre that derives its name from the Metroid and Castlevania game series, and usually includes these elements:
  • Open-ended worlds that emphasize returning to cleared areas with new abilities to unlock more sections
  • Progression locked behind certain weapon or movement upgrades
  • Collectathon-esque upgrades that enable a multitude of methods of powering up your character
Hollow Knight is not solely a Metroidvania, as it has elements from other genres, but it is it most significant genre for the purpose of comparison.
