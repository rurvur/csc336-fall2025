A Short Summary of the Development of Hollow Knight: Silksong


A nearly 7-year development time ends in a flash with an early September release

Announcement

Hollow Knight: Silksong was announced on February 14th, 2019. Since then the game has had 3 trailers:
  • A reveal trailer on 2/14/2019
  • A trailer for XBox Game Pass on 6/12/2022
  • A Release Trailer on 8/21/2025
Each trailer had at least a 3 year gap between each other, with little to no other information being released. The result was an extremely anxiety-ridden development cycle where fans of Hollow Knight speculated as to whether the game was still in the works.

You can get more info from this video on the topic!

A little information

Being a sequel to the 2017 Metroidvania Hollow Knight, Silksong already has en established following. While that may seem like a boon for the game, it makes the long development and periods of silence much more impactful, as it means there is already a dedicated following who have been waiting since its announcement. There's been roughly a 9 year gap between Hollow Knight's final update and Silksong' release, and the announcement of the game was nearly 7 years ago.
When you consider that this was originally planned as DLC for Hollow Knight, it becomes evident why the fanbase has been...antsy, to put it lightly.
The XBox Trailer
Nearly 3 years after reveal, in 2022, a while after COVID-19 put the industry on hold, Silksong got another new trailer announcing its inclusion in XBox GamePass on its release. While it unleashed a surge of interest from fans, in the long run this would serve as a benchmark for the next extreme stretch of silence. The first 3 year gap had the excuse of including the time in lockdown, this next 3 year gap would not have such an easy excuse.
