A Short Summary of the Development of Hollow Knight Silksong


A nearly 7-year development time ends in a flash with an early September release

Announcement

Hollow Knight: Silksong was announced on February 14th, 2019. Since then the game has had 3 trailers:
  • A reveal trailer on 2/14/2019
  • A trailer for XBox Game Pass on 6/12/2022
  • A Release Trailer on 8/21/2025
Each trailer had at least a 3 year gap between each other, with little to no other information being released. The result was an extremely anxiety-ridden development cycle where fans of Hollow Knight speculated as to whether the game was still in the works
